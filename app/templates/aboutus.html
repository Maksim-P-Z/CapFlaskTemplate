About Us (ME)


What is good my fellows? How very nice of you to come visit my site.


My name is Maksim Zarkovich. I am the creator of this magnificent geography website. As you can probably guess, I have quite a strong interest in geography. I love learning about the world, countries, cities, and people! (And how could I forget: Food too!)


Intended Purpose:


I built this website to form a geography community and to grow that community. My aim is to help people show their interet for geography, or if they don't have one, find their interest for geography.


Interactivity:


Currently, I have two types of users, geo-novices and geo-experts. I trust that you know yourself and don't feel the need to lie about this. Why you would want to do that? This will simply let other users get a little more context about you as they read your posts and comments!
